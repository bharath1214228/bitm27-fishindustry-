Fishing industry launched online
by The Fish Site
9 June 2004, at 1:00am

NAMIBIA - A namibian based website dedicated specifically to the country's second largest industry, the fishing and aquaculture sector, was launched at Walvis Bay last week. The website, officially launched by Interact Advertising Namibia, was unveiled by the Minister for Fisheries and Marine Resources Dr Abraham Iyambo at a function attended by key players in the industry.
